Update 2022-05-09

I bought myself a large panel (110W) and an easy MPPT. That is a little thing that stabilises the energy that comes out of the panel. Because the sun-shine fluctuates, and with it the energy-current, it can be problematic for the things you attach to the panel. This particular MPPT by revolt3 also has USB outlets, which is nice. If it produces to much power, maybe we can even charge some other devices.

I do need to learn how to set up the panel now, and if I miss anythingâ€¦ Oook I was missing a few things.
