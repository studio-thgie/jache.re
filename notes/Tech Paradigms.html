Tech Paradigms

It seems like, there are two fundamentally different paradigms at play within the development of tech. At one end we have tech designed to control. This paradigm is applied by the military industry, governement actants as well as profit-driven companies. On the other hand we have tech understood as infrastructural, meaning no life as we have it without tech; no us without tech.
